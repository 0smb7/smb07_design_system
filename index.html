Design systems serve as a comprehensive guide for creating cohesive, well-integrated user experiences. They are collections of reusable components, guided by clear standards, that can be assembled together to build any number of applications. A design system includes not just the visual style guide, but also coding standards, components, and other resources. They help teams to design and develop more efficiently by providing a common language and consistent branding across products. Design systems are not only about consistency; they also embody the principles and best practices of design and development, ensuring that products are accessible, responsive, and easy to use.
